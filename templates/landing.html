Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LeadBlitz

What is LeadBlitz? +
LeadBlitz is an AI-powered lead generation tool built specifically for freelance web designers and small agencies. It helps you find local businesses with outdated websites, scores their sites using AI, and generates professional audit reports you can send to win new clients.
How does LeadBlitz find leads? +
LeadBlitz searches Google Places to discover local businesses in your target area and industry. It then uses AI to score each business's website on a scale of 0-100, analyzing page speed, mobile-friendliness, SEO, SSL security, and overall design quality. Low-scoring businesses are your best prospects.
Is there a free trial? +
Yes! Every new account gets 200 free credits with no credit card required. That's enough to search for leads, score websites, and generate audit reports so you can see the full value of the platform before purchasing additional credits.
What does a website audit report include? +
Each audit report covers page speed and performance, mobile responsiveness, SEO fundamentals, SSL security status, and overall design quality. Reports are professionally branded and ready to send directly to potential clients as a PDF, making it easy to demonstrate your expertise and start a conversation.
Who is LeadBlitz designed for? +
LeadBlitz is built for freelance web designers, small web design agencies, and digital marketing consultants who do outbound prospecting. If you sell website design, redesign, or maintenance services to local businesses, LeadBlitz helps you find and convert the right prospects.
